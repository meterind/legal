Privacy Policy

Effective date: 9 May 2025  ·  Version 1.0

Meter ("the app", "we", "us") is an independent fare comparison tool for iOS. This policy explains what information Meter accesses, how it is used, and what we do not collect. We do not operate any backend servers. All data described below is processed locally on your device unless explicitly stated otherwise.

Short version: Meter does not collect personal data, does not create user accounts, and does not transmit your information to Meter-owned servers. Location and saved places stay on your device.

1. Information We Access

1a. Precise Location (GPS)

When you grant location permission, Meter reads your GPS coordinates to:

  • Suggest a pickup address (auto-filled in the pickup field).
  • Determine which city you are in so we can display a city label in the weather badge (e.g. "MUM" for Mumbai).
  • Fetch local weather via the Open-Meteo public API (see §3).

Location data is used in-session only. It is not stored persistently, not linked to any identifier, and not transmitted to any Meter-owned server.

Location permission is requested only when you tap the GPS button. You can deny it; the app works fully with manually typed addresses.

1b. Saved Places (Home / Work / Other)

If you save addresses (Home, Work, a custom place), they are stored on your device using iOS's standard app storage (AsyncStorage). They are never uploaded to any server.

1c. Onboarding Consent Flag

A single flag ("meter_onboarding_v1") is stored locally to remember that you have accepted these terms so the onboarding screen is not repeated.

1d. Provider Account Sessions (Uber, Ola)

If you sign in to Uber or Ola inside the app, your login is handled entirely by an embedded browser showing the provider's own website. Session cookies are stored by iOS's WebView engine, not by Meter. Meter cannot read your credentials, OTP, or account details. Meter reads only the publicly visible fare estimates that the provider's website renders.

Meter is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or a partner of Uber, Ola, or Rapido.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • Name, email address, phone number, or any identity.
  • Payment information or financial data.
  • Ride history or booking records.
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, UDID).
  • Crash logs, usage analytics, or behavioural data.
  • Advertising profiles of any kind.

Meter has no analytics SDK, no crash-reporting SDK, and no advertising SDK.

3. Third-Party Services We Contact

When you use Meter, your device makes network requests to the following third-party services. Each operates under its own privacy policy.

Service Purpose Data sent
Google Places API Address autocomplete search Search text you type
Google Directions API Road route between pickup and drop Pickup & drop coordinates
Google Maps SDK Map rendering Map tile requests (no personal data)
Open-Meteo Local weather display Approximate GPS coordinates
Comparify (cabs.comparify.pro) Rapido fare estimates Pickup & drop coordinates
m.uber.com Uber fare display (embedded browser) Pickup & drop coordinates (in URL)
book.olacabs.com Ola fare display (embedded browser) Pickup & drop coordinates (in URL)

Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy
Open-Meteo's privacy policy: open-meteo.com/en/terms

4. Data Retention

Meter stores only two items on your device:

  • Saved places — deleted when you remove the app or manually clear a saved place.
  • Onboarding flag — deleted when you remove the app.

We have no server-side data to delete because we collect none.

5. Children's Privacy

Meter is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through Meter, please contact us and we will investigate.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the effective date at the top and, where appropriate, notify users via an in-app notice on the next launch.
