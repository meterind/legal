Privacy Policy

Effective date: 9 May 2025  ·  Version 1.0

Meter ("the app", "we", "us") is an independent fare comparison tool for iOS. This policy explains what information Meter accesses and how it is used. Meter does not operate any backend servers. All processing happens on your device unless stated otherwise.

1. Information We Access

Location (GPS)

When you grant location permission, Meter reads your GPS coordinates to suggest a pickup address and display local weather. Location data is used in-session only — it is not stored, not linked to any identifier, and not sent to any Meter server. Location permission is only requested when you tap the GPS button. The app works fully without it.

Saved Places

If you save addresses (Home, Work, or a custom place), they are stored locally on your device and never uploaded to any server.

Consent Record

A flag is stored locally on your device to record that you have accepted these terms, so the consent screen is not shown again. It is deleted when you remove the app.

Provider Sessions

If you sign in to a ride provider within the app, your login is handled entirely by the provider's own website shown in a secure embedded browser. Meter does not have access to your credentials, OTP, or account details. Meter is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or a partner of Uber, Ola, or Rapido.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • Name, email, phone number, or any personal identifier
  • Payment or financial information
  • Ride history or booking records
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash data

Meter does not use analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting services.

3. Third-Party Services

Meter uses third-party services to provide maps, address search, weather, and fare estimates. Your pickup and drop coordinates may be transmitted to these services as required to fulfil the app's functions. Each third-party service operates under its own privacy policy. Meter is not responsible for third-party data practices.

4. Data Retention

Meter stores only your saved places and consent record — both on your device only. Both are deleted when you uninstall the app. We hold no server-side data.

5. Children's Privacy

Meter is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If you believe a minor has used Meter, contact us and we will take appropriate action.

6. Indian Data Protection

Meter complies with applicable Indian data protection law, including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDPA). As Meter does not collect or process personal data on its servers, no data fiduciary obligations arise. Location data processed on-device is not transmitted to or stored by Meter.

7. Changes to This Policy

We will update the effective date if we make material changes and, where appropriate, notify you via an in-app notice on next launch.
